HOW TO MAKE FLUFFY PANCAKES

This easy recipe makes pancakes that are light and fluffy.
You only need a few simple ingredients that you probably have in your kitchen right now.

PANCAKE INGREDIENTS

  1. Flour
  2. Sugar
  3. Milk
  4. Eggs
  5. Salt
  6. Baking powder

PROCEDURE

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl, i.e flour, baking powder, sugar and salt.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients in another bowl, i.e milk, water and eggs
  3. Combine both the mixtures and whisk till fully combined
  4. Heat a large pan over medium heat.
  5. Lightly brush pan with butter
  6. Pour the mixture on the pan till the edges are dry the turnover
  7. Once flipped cook for another 1-2 minutes
  8. enjoy!
